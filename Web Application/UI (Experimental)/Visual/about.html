Welcome to Eden Farms! We're an In-Development Smart Verical Farming project with big ambitions and even bigger dreams.
We pride ourselves on using the best available computing, software, machine components and personnel to make our vision a reality - to revolutionize agriculture.
Our mission is to create a Smart Vertical Farming system that is capable of efficently farming plants and crops to produce the best fruits, vegetables, dairy products and more, while minimalizing costs, pollution and manual labour.
We're still small in scale of... everthing, but just like a plant needs love and care to grow, so will we with the right support.
